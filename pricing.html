Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for printing?
You can add credits to your DocoPrint account using UPI, credit/debit cards, or campus cash. The amount will be deducted when you confirm your order.
How long does it take to get my prints?
Standard orders are typically ready for pickup within 30-60 minutes depending on the location. Express service can reduce this to 15 minutes.
Can I get a refund if there's an issue with my print?
Yes, if there's a quality issue with your print that is our fault, we'll reprint it for free or refund your credits. Just report the issue within 24 hours.
What file formats do you accept?
We accept most common file formats including PDF, DOCX, PPTX, JPG, PNG, and more. PDF is recommended for best results.
